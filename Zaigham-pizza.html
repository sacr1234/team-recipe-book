BBQ terayaki pizza

By: Abdullah Zaigham

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara sauce
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella)
  • Chicken or sausage

Instructions:

  1. Knead the dough and flatten it out on a surface
  2. Place the toppings with a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom, then cheese, and lastly the chicken and BBQ sauce
  3. Place the pizza in the oven at 450Â°F for 12-15 minutes

Cooking time: 90 minutes

Serves: 10 people
